# Update Your Developer Profile

> **Source**: https://developer.intuit.com/app/developer/qbo/docs/get-started/developer-account-settings

## Overview

Your Intuit Developer Profile lets you:
- Sign in to the Intuit Developer Portal
- Create and store apps
- Manage development-related info

It also has basic contact info we use to keep in touch with you, such as your email address, mailing address, and phone number.

---

## Update Your Profile

1. [Sign in](https://developer.intuit.com/dashboard) to your developer account.
2. Select **My Hub > Account** from the upper-right corner of the toolbar.
3. Make your updates.

> **Tip**: We recommend using the same email address for communications and the User ID you use to sign in to the portal.
