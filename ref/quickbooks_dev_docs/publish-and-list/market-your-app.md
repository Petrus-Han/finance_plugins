# Market your app

Now that your app is [listed in the QuickBooks App Store](/app/developer/qbo/docs/go-live/list-on-the-app-store), we want to help you with it's long-term success.

There are many ways to get the word out about your app. We encourage you to advertise and promote your app through multiple channels.

Here are tips for putting your best foot forward while staying within our marketing and advertising guidelines.

## List on the QuickBooks App Store

Listing in the QuickBooks App Store potentially puts your app in front of millions of QuickBooks Online users. It's one of the easiest ways to get exposure.

If you haven't already, learn [how to list your app in the QuickBooks App Store](/app/developer/qbo/docs/go-live/list-on-the-app-store).

## Review marketing, design, and branding guidelines

When you fill out your app card, you may notice recommendations about logos, app naming conventions, and certain trademarks. It's important you keep these requirements in mind as you promote your app on various platforms.

- [Name and logo guidelines for apps](/app/developer/qbo/docs/go-live/list-on-the-app-store/naming-and-logo-guidelines)
- [Marketing requirements for apps](/app/developer/qbo/docs/go-live/publish-app/marketing-requirements)
- [Search engine marketing and advertising guidelines](/app/developer/qbo/docs/go-live/market-your-app/search-engine-marketing-and-advertising-guidelines)

## Promote your app

Here are a great ways you can get the word out:

- Encourage users to [write and share reviews](/app/developer/qbo/docs/go-live/market-your-app/ratings-and-reviews) on the QuickBooks App Store.
- Reach out and introduce your app to [ProAdvisors and influencers](/app/developer/qbo/docs/go-live/market-your-app/reach-out-to-quickbooks-proadvisors).
- Follow our search engine marketing guidelines and [market with tools like Google AdWords](/app/developer/qbo/docs/go-live/market-your-app/search-engine-marketing-and-advertising-guidelines).
- Share testimonials and reviews on social media.
- Send out a [press release](/app/developer/qbo/docs/go-live/market-your-app/public-relations-guidelines).

## Marketing opportunities with Intuit

Eligibility for marketing or promotions with Intuit depends on several factors, such as the popularity of your app with QuickBooks Online customers.

We're always on the lookout for great apps that solve big small business problems. Primarily, we look for apps with:

- Thousands of active users (joint customers)
- Many five star reviews
- High conversion rate from your listing on on the QuickBooks App Store
- High retention rates
- Low API error rates
- High growth rates

## Consider going global

QuickBooks has official app stores in the following countries:

- Australia
- Canada
- India
- United Kingdom
- United States

You list in as many app stores as you'd like, but each listing requires its own review. App stores are region-specific. This helps connect your app with the right audience.

Keep these tips in mind if you have a global audience:

- Test your app with [regionally-specific versions of QuickBooks Online](https://quickbooks.intuit.com/choose-country/) for each country you plan to launch in.
- You may need to provide support for users in multiple time zones.
- Consider localizing your documentation for each country you support.
- Clearly define the currencies your app supports. This should include the home (default) currency and any special vendor, customer, or employee currencies. Learn [how to manage multiple currencies](/app/developer/qbo/docs/workflows/manage-multiple-currencies) in your app.
- Transaction objects implement legitimate tax structures. Learn more about [the global tax model](/app/developer/qbo/docs/workflows/calculate-sales-tax/automated-sales-tax-for-non-us-locales).
- Use supported character encoding. US versions of QuickBooks Online support **ISO-8859-1 (extended ASCII)** character encoding. Non-US versions of QuickBooks Online support **UTF-8** character encoding.

## Keep your app compliant

Besides just promotion, take proactive steps so you're always ready for the annual technical and security review. All apps listed in the QuickBooks App Store must go through the annual review on the anniversary of publication.

We do reviews to make sure apps continue meeting our marketing, technical, and security. Here's [how to maintain compliance year-round](/app/developer/qbo/docs/go-live/list-on-the-app-store/maintaining-compliance).
