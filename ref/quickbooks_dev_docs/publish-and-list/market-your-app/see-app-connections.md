# See current app connections

Want to see how many users connect to your app each month? Here's how to get a quick report.

## Review an app connection report

1. [Sign in](https://developer.intuit.com/dashboard) to your developer account.
2. Select **My Hub > App dashboard** from the upper-right corner of the toolbar.
3. Select and open the app to check.
4. In the **Production** section, select **Connections**.
5. Select **Analytics** from the left navigation panel.

The Connections Report shows you basic connection trends over the last 6 months.

> **Tip**: If you go to the **Development** section and select **Connections**, you'll see how many times you've connected to your sandbox environments.
