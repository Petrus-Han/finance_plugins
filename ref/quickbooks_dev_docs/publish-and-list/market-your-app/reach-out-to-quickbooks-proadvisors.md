# Market your app to ProAdvisors

Millions of small businesses who use QuickBooks Online [rely on ProAdvisors](https://quickbooks.intuit.com/accountants/proadvisor/) for accounting advice and training.

The ProAdvisor Program is the largest network of professional accounting and small business advisors who specialize in QuickBooks. Most are certified CPAs, accountants, bookkeepers, and small business consultants. Their expertise spans the Intuit Financial Ecosystem.

With such deep insights, ProAdvisors are key influencers in the accounting industry. They're also expert guides for small business owners. As you create your marketing plan, consider reaching out to ProAdvisors.

## Reach out to ProAdvisors

Visit the [Find an Accountant](https://quickbooks.intuit.com/find-an-accountant/) page and search by industry or business type, location, and other details. Connect with Proadvisors who are a great fit for your app.

Introduce them to your app and tell them why it's a great solution. Consider offering them special pricing or training. Work with and support them so there's a mutual benefit.

> **Important**: If you haven't already, [set up Intuit Single Sign-on](/app/developer/qbo/docs/develop/authentication-and-authorization/single-sign-on-models) for your app. When you [list it on the QuickBooks App Store](/app/developer/qbo/docs/go-live/list-on-the-app-store), enable the **Accountant ready** option so your app appears in the Apps tab in QuickBooks Online Accountant. This lets ProAdvisors quickly find and recommend it to their clients.
